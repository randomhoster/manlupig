Privacy Policy

At Twin Ei8ht Resort, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website or avail of our services.

We may collect personal information including, but not limited to:

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

3. Protection of Data

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Only authorized resort personnel have access to your information for service purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Information may only be shared if required by law or in cases where it is necessary to fulfill your booking or request (e.g., payment processing).

5. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These cookies do not collect personal data and can be disabled through your browser settings if preferred.

We reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page and are effective immediately upon posting.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy, feel free to contact us through our Contact Page.

By using this website and our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. Thank you for trusting Twin Ei8ht Resort!
